Chicken with butter sauce

Ingredients

  • 600gr skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 3 teaspoon butter
  • 1 lemmon
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Wash the chicken breasts
  2. Add salt, pepper and some lemon drops to chicken breasts on both sides
  3. Add a little butter in a preheated frying pan and place the chicken on the pan with the melted butter.
  4. Leave cooking for 10 minutes.

Orange Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 10 cubes ice

Directions

  1. In a blender, combine orange juice concentrate, milk, water, vanilla, sugar and ice.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
